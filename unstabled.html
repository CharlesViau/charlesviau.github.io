About The Project

Description

Tired of rural farming life and yearning for something more, Our brave goat sets out for adventure. With a stalwart hamster companion and a can-do attitude, Can he ford the dangerous rocky roads ahead and Unstable. This is an infinite runner game. This project was made in 48 hours, during a Ludum Dare game jam. You can see the comments and review on the jam page of our game by clicking here.

My Role

I was responsible of creating all the sounds assets needed for the project, from SFX to music. I also was responsible to implement all the sounds in unity using various techniques such as making the sounds play by code, unity events or implementing it directly in the animation. It was my first project implementing sounds and using unity.
